ABOUT ORGANISATION

Palluyirkalam, meaning bio-diversity farming is designed and established by Mr. Rajkumar Murugesan since 2020. He owns an event management company since 1992 and also works as art director in the Indian film industry. Our farm is designed with the aim of supporting all the lives around us.

MISSION

Here at Palluyirkalam, we strive to produce the organic food of best quality food. In addition to producing the best quality food, we want everyone to know where it is coming from and how it is grown. We allow every one visit our farm, helping understand the process and replicate this at your place.

We want to encourage more families to incorporate organic produce into their lives leading to a healthy life and; we believe, by providing a quality product and educating everyone about will help accomplish this.

VISION

  • To provide access to the organic consumables at the reasonable price
  • To educate healthy life habits to the next generation
  • To provide a platform for the small and medium farmers to sell their products
  • To provide a peaceful and sustainable atmosphere to the life around us
