ABOUT ORGANISATION

Palluyir kalam is a project designed for Biodiversity Farming, generates income from various agriculture associated activities. We are not focused just on profits but wish to share the happiness with all lives around. This project is designed and established by Mr. Rajkumar Murugesan since 2020. He owns an event management company since 1992 and also works as art director in the Indian film industry. He designed this project with the aim of supporting the farmers and also allow the other lives around us to lead a happy life.
