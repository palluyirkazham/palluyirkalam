FARM TOURISM

We are located at Idaikattur, that has historical significance. It is an auspicious land where Siddhar Idaikadar lived and did farming. From sacred texts, it is strongly believed that during his period, he has helped overcome severe famine by re-arranging the planets positions restoring prosperity, happiness and peace.

Additionally, a French missionary church known for its religious importance and structural beauty. Built with around 200 varieties of tiles and moulded bricks, the church hosts a mesmerizing architecture.

A pleasant stay at our farm, would help you experience the uniqueness of the farm with easy access to these auspicious destinations; Also, we help you with access to other infamous spots of historical importance and other interests.

FARM PRODUCTS

All the farm produced from our farm is sold at reasonable market price. Additionally we sell organic products from our small and marginal farmer friends around our locations, helping them make farming profitable for everyone. We ensure quality of their products during procurement.

We also sell handi-crafts made by the local. They are made from clay, coconut shell and other organic disposables. We ensure whatever sold from our shop is environment-friendly.

ABOUT ORGANISATION

Palluyirkalam, meaning bio-diversity farming is designed and established by Mr. Rajkumar Murugesan since 2020. He owns an event management company since 1992 and also works as art director in the Indian film industry. Our farm is designed with the aim of supporting all the lives around us.
